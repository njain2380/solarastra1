Frequently Asked Questions

1. How long does the solar installation process take? +

Typically, once permits are secured, the physical installation on your rooftop takes between 2 to 4 days, depending on the system size and roof complexity. The entire process from initial consultation to commissioning usually spans 4 to 8 weeks, primarily due to utility approvals.

2. What happens to the solar power if I'm not home? (Net Metering) +

In India, most rooftop solar systems are connected to the grid via **Net Metering**. Any excess electricity your panels generate is automatically fed back into the utility grid, and you receive a credit on your electricity bill for that energy. You only pay the utility for the 'net' amount of electricity you consume.

3. Do you assist with subsidies and financing/loan options? +

Absolutely. We are a one-stop-shop. Our team helps you navigate the complex process of securing Central Financial Assistance (CFA) subsidies from the government. We also have established partnerships with leading financial institutions to provide competitive loan options, ensuring you can convert your electricity bill into a sustainable EMI.

4. What is the typical lifespan of a Solarastra system? +

The solar panels we use are designed to last 25 years or more, with guaranteed performance output. Inverters typically have a lifespan of 10-15 years, and their replacement cost is minimal compared to the savings generated over time. We provide clear warranties for all components.
